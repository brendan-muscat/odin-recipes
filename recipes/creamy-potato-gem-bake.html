Creamy Potato Gem Bake

Description

Hereâ€™s an easy, cheesy bake for your couch potatoes.

Ingredients

  • 400g frozen potato gems
  • 30g butter
  • 1 1/2 tbsp plain flour
  • 250ml (1 cup) milk
  • 80g (1 cup) coarsely grated cheddar
  • Chopped crispy bacon, to serve
  • Fresh chives, chopped, to serve

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 200C/180C fan forced. Arrange the potato gems on a large baking tray and bake for 20 minutes or until crisp. Set aside to cool slightly.
  2. Meanwhile, melt the butter in a 20cm (base measurement) ovenproof frying pan over medium heat. Add the flour and cook, stirring, for 1 minute. Gradually add the milk, stirring well after each addition until well combined, until smooth. Bring to a simmer and cook for 2 minutes. Add the cheddar and stir until melted.
  3. Arrange the potato gems over the cheese sauce in the pan. Bake for 10 minutes or until bubbling. Sprinkle with the bacon and chives to serve.
